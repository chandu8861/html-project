About TMC

TMC was founded in 2020. Our Goal is to brings the authentic taste of Karnataka's Malnad region to every cup 🍵🍵🍵. Inspired by traditional coffee culture, we serve rich and aromatic filter coffee made from carefully selected beans. Our cafe is a place where people can relax, connect, and enjoy the true flavor of Malnad coffee.

Coffee Trivia

Where is most of Karnataka's coffee grown?

Most of Karnataka's coffee is grown in the Malnad region, especially in Chikkamagaluru, Kodagu (Coorg), and Hassan districts. These areas have ideal climate, rainfall, and elevation for coffee cultivation. (Source: Coffee Board of India )
